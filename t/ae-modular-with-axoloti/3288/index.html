so my AE modular has just arrived, its a budget modular (~ 30 euro/module!) which Im going to be doing some serious integration with axoloti with .... hence recent questions.

I think its a great match for axoloti since a) its cheap b) sounds great ... and works 0..5v which is pretty simple to interface to axoloti 0..3.3v.
being 'cheap' means for electronics newbies like me, I'm not worried about blowing up $$$ of gear by connecting up axoloti... and i also see axoloti as being able to easily add lots of value.
(unlike eurorack where you could just modules off the shelf, to add the value!)

anyway, Ive put together a couple of videos, both using axoloti simply for reverb and midi interfacing... (deeper integration is 'on its ways')
