Gavin 2018-04-28 07:36:50 UTC #2

Although I cannot see what you have done, I can only assume that if you have the LED connected directly to the pot, then to the Axo, this won't work unles you are using a double gang port and the led is connected to the other gang. Or you could try a connecting the led to a different GPIO on the Axo, digital out, but have an object blink the led at different rates depending on the pots position.
