Well,

First I need to state that I´m no expert in the area whatsoever. But it isn´t as easy as one might think initially. Given that you have an audio source where you easily can tap the beat along to it: try figuring out what in that audio source you are actually following. Is it an separate instrument, or a 'composite' beat of several instruments together? Which frequencies are the main part of that beat/tempo?

And what is supposed to happen when there are no beat present (ambient audio)?
