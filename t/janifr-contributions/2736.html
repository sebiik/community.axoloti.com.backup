Hi,

I just uploaded my version of a drawbar organ to the community library. Key features at this point are full polyphony with all 91 voices, 2nd/3rd harmonic percussion, pop-free note-ons and note-offs using weighted averaging of gain per sample and upper foldback. I also implemented some sort of key-klick emulation but it's not very good at the moment.
