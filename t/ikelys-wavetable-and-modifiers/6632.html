This is my current project.
A wavetable synth based on the tiar/XT objects.

it features

  • 16 different wavetables. The wavetable excursion is controlled by a ramp generator with two rates (that can be positive or negative) that can be synched to the note on.
  • 16 different "modifier sequences" that will modify the wavetable though diverse kinds of modulations.
  • a low pass filter
  • chorus
  • delay
  • an oled scope
  • 4 voice polyphony
