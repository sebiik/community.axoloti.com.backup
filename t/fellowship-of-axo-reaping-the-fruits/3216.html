Fellowship of Axo - 2 of 2

This is the second part of the Fellowship of Axo challenge.

In this part we take 'seeds' from part one of this challenge , and tend and nurture them, and the reap the fruits ... or in plain speak simply take one or more seeds from Seeds of inspiration and use it as inspiration for a track

submission date : open ended, feel free to pick a seed and develop it, and put the post the results here.

Restrictions

only rule is you have to use at least one seed, detail here which seed(s) you used.

apart from that no rules. you can use the seed 'as is' ,or mangle/edit/change the seed beyond the point of recognition. you can use whatever you want to produce the track, e.g. daws/synths/fx - as long as Axoloti plays some part in the track.

Submissions

post entries in this thread - either as soundcloud or youtube links.
include a reference to the seed(s) you used in the track

note: it looks like everyone is busy, so Ive decided to make this an 'open ended' challenge, with no end date, so you are free to add new seeds or new fruits at any time. the more seeds added, the more fruit we can grow
