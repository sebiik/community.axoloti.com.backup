ProtoZOA by AmeNote is the official MIDI 2.0 spec development board from the MIDI Association. Featuring a dual Raspberry Pi Pico combined with a CME WIDI Core it is supported by a suite of open-source firmware and software. https://amenote.com/?page_id=74
