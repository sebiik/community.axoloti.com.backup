Gavin 2019-04-23 23:07:55 UTC #2

Instead of desoldering the USB port, leave it there, but instead, solder on lead wires to the positive and ground connections on the USB port. Or, hack half a USB cable, plug it into the port, and on the other end disseminate the wires to connect power.
