I have been really enjoying my Axo for learning syntheses and effects, etc.My next little project is to make a standalone talk-box carrier.

I have a heil talkbox and it needs a source to feed the tube. I used one of my synths in the past but now I think it would be cool to have a dedicated box just for the Heil. To boost the signal I am using a smokey amp (smokeyamps.com). I am going thru the osc/saw's and testing to see which I prefer. I'd like to use my launchpad mini instead of a standard keyboard but I am still fumbling around with how to get it to play all the notes.

If anyone is interested, I'll post a finished (or in progress) video.
