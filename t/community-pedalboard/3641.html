Hi everybody.
Inspired by this thread http://community.axoloti.com/t/general-midi-collaborative-library/2545 , I thought I'd start to publish some guitar oriented FX I patched with axoloti. I made them community-friendly (I HOPE!), a subpatch like a good old stompbox, with ins and outs, relevant controls and a switch. I used many community objects from @rbrt, @lokki, @TheSlowGrowth and many others...
I'd like YOU to use this thread to post your effects in a similar format, so to inspire others and make a lovely virtual stomp boxes archive for the posterity.
Cheers
