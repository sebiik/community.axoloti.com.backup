DrJustice 2016-06-06 23:15:13 UTC #2

I'd say waveshaping is the way to go. If you're using drj/lfo/lfo_sync, there's pwm, triangle saw, and sine which can all be shaped by a few simple modules. E.g. the bipolar triangle into a math/sin, sine through math/wrap or multiplying saw and tri with a math/* will give you something already, the saw can be used to look up tables and so on. I'm sure there are purpose made wave shaper objects in the libraries as well.
