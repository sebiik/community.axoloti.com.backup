Gavin 2017-01-26 09:28:27 UTC #2

You want to have a good look at the signal going into to Axo.
If you use a Bipolar Scope Display from the object list, connect the signal direct from input, you will see if your signal is clipping anywhere.
It doesn't take much to get a clipping signal from the guitar.
Also check your audio input, don't use the "audio/config" object. Can always add gain down the line.
Also check anything that might be boosting the signal before it gets to the Axo.

Why are you using a stereo to 2 x mono cables ? all you need is a mono from the guitar to the input.
