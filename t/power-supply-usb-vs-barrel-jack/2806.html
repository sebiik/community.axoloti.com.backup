johannes 2017-02-07 18:01:14 UTC #5

Axoloti will stop drawing power from the device power when there is power on the DC barrel socket.
BUT Axoloti reports itself to the USB Host as a device that drinks power from the USB bus, even if it gets power from the DC barrel socket.
Maybe feed external power into the USB Hub instead of the Axoloti Core (respecting the power requirements of the USB hub)?
