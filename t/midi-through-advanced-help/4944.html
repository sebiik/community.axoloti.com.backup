you know midi/in/keyb - what channel does that look at? Just channel one?

janvantomme 2018-09-06 18:10:07 UTC #4

Default patch channel is 1, but you can change that in the patch settings (View -> Settings).

If you place the midi/in/keyb in a monophonic subpatch, you change the midi channel of that subpatch. So you can have multiple subpatches, each listening to their own assigned MIDI channel.
