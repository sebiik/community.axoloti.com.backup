A patch I've been working on for a while, inspired by the Korg Volca Bass.

BA-SuperBass.axp (19.7 KB)

There are three oscillators, tuned to C3, C4 and C5. Don't touch the dials!

Things you can do to create interesting basslines:

  • Choose between Square and Saw for each oscillator.
  • Oscillator 2 & 3 can be turned on/off
  • Choose the LFO type (Square, Saw or SIne)
  • Change the Intensity of the LFO
  • Choose the target of the LFO (Off, Amp, Filter Pitch, Filter Resonance)
  • Filter can be turned on/off
  • Distortion can be turned on/off

This is what the patch looks like. Have fun playing with it.
