johannes 2015-10-08 21:39:14 UTC #3

After using math/exp as a waveshaper on a sine-wave, the signal is heavily asymmetrical and has a strong DC offset. Changing gain on such signal reveals zipper noise more than on many other signals.

The mix objects do not interpolate (or camouflage zipper noise) at all.
A way around would be using vca objects, and "const/dial p" for midimapped control. Further smoothing can be obtained with a k-rate filter.
