We're organizing another synth-event at our hacklab in Amsterdam.
There is room for discussion, presentations and hands-on tinkering.
Expect some soldering, patching, tweaking, and noodling of audio waves that tickle your ear.

3 days of playing with audio stuff from diy electronics to patch environments like Pd and Axoloti.
On saturday we'll also have food in the local community kitchen.

Hope to see you there
