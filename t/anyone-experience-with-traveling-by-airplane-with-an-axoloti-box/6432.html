servandisco 2020-02-22 15:22:05 UTC #4

I always carry a battery pack and headphones so I could boot the axoloti and make it run a self playing patch or something.. If you can prove that it's an audio device you have less chances of getting in trouble.. I never needed to take my Axolotis out of their box or the backpack though..
Btw. one of the rules of the TSA is that if you carry a computer, this must be able to boot in order to get on board, so, try to leave some charge in the battery and take the charger in carry on. (they are also forbidden in checked baggage in some companies).
good luck!
