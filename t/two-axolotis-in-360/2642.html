I've been thinking of the most expressive and portable setup I can make with my different Axo boxes.
I find that two axo's and a few controllers is the best combination. Here are a few videos of such setups.
It all fits in a sports bag (with audio and video recording equipment and my small roland mobile cube) and a guitar sac (for the ribbon synth)
The other setup is my double axo keyboard, which is a bit bigger, but has a nice 61 full size keyboard.

These videos explore using one axo as a 5 track delay machine (and short reverb) and using the other as the sound source, with patch changing and things.

I'm testing 360° videos too (put at highest resolution to try and see something), saves me having to think where to aim the camera
