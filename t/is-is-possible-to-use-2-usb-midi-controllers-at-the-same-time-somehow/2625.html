janvantomme 2016-12-09 15:49:32 UTC #2

Using 2 USB MIDI controllers won't be possible, as Axoloti doesn't support USB hubs.

iPhone with Lemur + 1 USB MIDI controller should work. But your patch needs to be uploaded as startup patch, since the iPhone will power the Axoloti.
