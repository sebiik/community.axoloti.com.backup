Dear all, I decided to design a case for the AxoControl from the ground up and get it laser cut and after ages and ages in AutoCAD, finally got it right and sent it of to Duke Studios in Leeds UK [https://duke-studios.com] to get it snipped out in 3m clear acrylic. The midi-sockets stick up on the board so rather than design a slot on the case for them or raise top to miss them I trimmed them off and the whole thing looks neater. More than happy to get the .dwg, .dxf or .pdf file out there if there is an easy way and if anyone wants them
