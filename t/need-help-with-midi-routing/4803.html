jaffasplaffa 2018-07-23 07:14:47 UTC #2

The midi outputs are easy, as they have selector for channel and device, so you simply just set the midi out object to the right channel and device port.

For the midi inputs, they don't have a midi channel and device selector, so you have to do something else here. You could make a subpatch, set to a dedicated midi channel and midi port. like this and then connect it to the midi output
