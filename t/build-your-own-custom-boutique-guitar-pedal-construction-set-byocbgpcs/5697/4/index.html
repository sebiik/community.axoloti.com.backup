DIYDSP 2019-05-10 19:38:39 UTC #1

I was just building myself a custom boutique guitar pedal with an Axoloti when I thought, "Hey aren't there a lot of people who want to use this?" Axoloti is soooo good at helping designers who have great ideas, but no time/space/tools/etc to make boutique pedals. Since I have a little extra time on my hands right now, I'd like to discuss the design of a common HW platform that could would have the features the majority of people would want to use!

I'm imagining us using it for our own music, for that curious friend who always asks, "Hey can you make me a pedal that does something crazy like generator notes in a key signature in time to the tap tempo" and even make your own small run of boutique pedals!

I'm willing to take on this project! B) So I'd like to hear from others what controls/features they want in it. For example, how many knobs, what size knobs? How many switches? how many tap switches? Should there be a display? A selector switch? What other features would you want?
