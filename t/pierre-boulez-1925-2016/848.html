Such an important artist of the 20th century... RIP

So I decided to do a very modest "tribute" patch.
It's a self-playing thing, that uses two samples that need to be in the root of the SD card
Only two main commands, start button, and volume (and tempo if you want).
As it needs to charge the samples into tables, it needs some time to start up, and produces a freezing sound just after you click on the "Start" button (and it freezes the software during those long seconds too)
Normally the volume level should start off at 0 to stay safe, but check in case !
