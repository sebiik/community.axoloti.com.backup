Hi all,

Just finished off my loop mangling factotum patch. It has a 4 bar looper going into 2 factotums, each with a filter and a midi Looper (7 slots, 4 bars each).
There are envelopes for pitch and stretch and envelopes for each factotum's vca. The factotums also work on the live input.

There's also 2x 4 bar automation loopers that record any parameter changes, and 16 preset slots.

Parameters are controlled by a launch control xl and factotums are 'played' with a launchpad.

The two 4x4 grids on the right play the factotums with pads set to different settings (different octaves/speeds/directions). When you play a pad it records and then loops for as long as you hold the pad down. Recording length is set with a knob and can go from tiny unquantized lengths to lengths quantized in 16ths. Factotum loops can be up to a bar long. You can also change it so it holds the last recording and the pads just change pitch/direction.

The two columns on the right are the clipslots for recording the factotum grids.

The two orange columns are the presets.

Here's the patch-

malcolms grandaughter.axp (282.1 KB)

The patch won't work standalone as it's part of a 3 axoloti setup... Bass-station Looper, synced delay, chorus, reverb and a drum machine.
