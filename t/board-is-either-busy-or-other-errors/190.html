johannes 2015-09-01 09:40:30 UTC #11

Uninstall the driver for "Axoloti Core (Interface 0)". This interface is a MIDI device, you seem to have installed the WinUSB or LibUSB driver for this interface. After uninstalling and plug and play again this interface should automatically install as a "Sound, video and game controller".
