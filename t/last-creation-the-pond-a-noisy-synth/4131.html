clerphil 2018-01-18 15:55:02 UTC #1

Here is my last creation, a little synth that mimics the noises of a pound fauna ( insects, birds, batracians ).
Enclosed in a cigar box that I have painted and with an Axoloti Core inside, of course.
Only 4 buttons.
2 for volume of left and right audio output and 2 for variations of the sounds.
Very slow LFOs change the parameters in a constant and unpredictable way.
Here is the patch, very simple as you can see.

It is quite complicated because they study how birds compose songs as sequences of "words", i.e. modulated sounds. These songs can be used in different contexts, for example to recognize birds of the same species or even birds of the same "family" (singing an almost similar repertoire).
