Hi all,

I've been busy working on some new objects and thought I'd share this. Objects are a work in progress, but close. This patch is stable.

It's a modulated, synced delay. No clicks, but you might get some pitch drift if your clock is unstable.

Delay time can be synced to midi or tap tempo, with a knob running through straight and dotted divisions. So, 1 bar, *1/2 bar, 1/2 bar, *1/4 bar, 1/4 bar, etc, etc. Or, can run unsynced.

Modulation is from a multi wave lfo (triangle, sine, square, saw). There is a pulse width control that affects all wave shapes. The lfo can be synced to tap/midi with a similar division knob set up, or be unsynced.

There's also a sample and hold mode on the lfo - it runs synced to midi/tap, at a separate division to the lfo. It holds whatever the lfo is doing, but at it's own division. If you turn the lfo/modulation division knob to max it produces noise, and the sample and hold becomes random.

The modulation depth is controlled by a bipolar knob, and it's scaled according to the delay time. The over all range of the depth knob can be increased/decreased with a few buttons, going from subtle to crazy.

It has a low pass filter in the delay feedback path because it sounds nice.
