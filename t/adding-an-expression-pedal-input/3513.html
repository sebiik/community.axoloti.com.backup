janvantomme 2017-07-17 09:08:22 UTC #2

I wouldn't solder it directly to the Axoloti. Best to buy a stereo female jack to solder to the Axoloti so you can still use your pedal with other things. I wouldn't worry too much about the 50k pot, it should work. The values I've used in the book are the ones Johannes suggested on the forum when he first shared the basic schematics on how to connect things.

Guess this will be how you need to connect everything to the Axoloti:

Black wire: GND
Red wire: VDDA
White wire: Analog input
