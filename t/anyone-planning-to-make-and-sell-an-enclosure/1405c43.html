Wooden enclosure for AXOLOTI CORE SYNTH and Raspberry Pi 3 B+ boards.

s-l1600.jpg1440x842

The design of this case is made to not have to use many screws.

A pre-cut has been made in the lid in order to add Rotary Encoder.

Screw and nuts 10mm long and 3mm in diameter
