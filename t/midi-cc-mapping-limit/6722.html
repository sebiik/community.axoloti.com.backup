cire 2020-07-10 15:08:14 UTC #6

Looks like you've reached the sRAM limit.
maybe your patch is too big. Try reorganize your objects so that cables always go from left to right, and top to bottom. This can save quite some ram, especially red and blue ones.
CC assign take ressources, so that might help you have more.

cire 2020-07-10 15:10:54 UTC #7

also if you don't use preset or modulation sources, you can put everything to zero in view/settings. I think that reduces ressources too.
