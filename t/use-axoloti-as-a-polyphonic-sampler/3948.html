Is it possible do create a polyphonic sampler with an axoloti?

jfcharles 2017-11-21 15:09:22 UTC #2

Sure, it is possible. I would first make a monophonic sampler: look at the table/play pitch object and its help file. After it works, I'd turn it into an embedded polyphonic patcher (object patch/patcher), as for instance in patch File->Library->factory->demos->synth->electric piano.
