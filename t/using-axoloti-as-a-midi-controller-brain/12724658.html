here is a short vid tutorial about arduino uno, pots and midi CC.

If you're going to use a arduino, I would recommend to have a look at the teensy LC. It's cheap and it also works as an USB-midi device. An arduino uno can also act as a usb-midi device with the help of HIDUINO or MOCO:

A midi-box MIDIO 128 V3 or MIDIbox NG will do the job without any coding mods. And If your using the STM Disco board as maincore you can later use your disco board also with axoloti FW.

I would prefer a midibox or arduino based solution. For arduinos the main work will be copy/paste and edit your code for the need of your 64pot project.
