watamacha 2018-03-18 17:14:13 UTC #2

do you have an interp object after the table/play? also, your samples should be recorded at a high enough sample rate that your lowest pitch is 48k effective rate and everything else is higher pitched (and thus higher rate). e.g. if you want to go as low as C3 with a C4 sample, the sample should be recorded at 96k so that the effective rate when played at C3 is 48k.
