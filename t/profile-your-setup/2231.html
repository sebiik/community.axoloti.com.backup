It would be handy for support purposes if users included details of their setup in their forum profile.

to do: click on your profile, top right icon , then select 'gear' icon (preferences), fill details in the 'about me' field... you can of course add other interesting info too if you wish

most useful is the operating system your using, and its version e.g. Mac OSX 10.11.6, Windows 10
(other hardware controllers etc, might be useful too...)

then with one click on your badge we (and other users) can see which what your setup is, very useful if a question or issue about the Axoloti UI is related to your operating system etc.
(its easy to forget to include this in posts, so we end up asking, and this takes an extra post or two to clear up)
