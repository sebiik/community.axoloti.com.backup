What's up Axoloti fam. Here's my first Axoloti synthesizer - Zagausiq. My goal was to boil a subtractive synthesizer down to all the fun and reactive functions that can be easily understood while giving enough control and variability to make unique sounds that can evolve. I designed the icons to communicate the functions I intended but also made them ambiguous enough so you can change function. Made from walnut, ash, ebony, cork and richlite.
