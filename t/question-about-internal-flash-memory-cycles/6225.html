johannes 2019-12-19 00:08:55 UTC #2
mastaman:

I wonder what actually happens when i do Upload to SDcard as startup? Will it then flash the internal mem with the patch file from the SD card every time i boot up the Axoloti in standalone mode? Taking one of the 10 000 write cycles on each boot?

I understand your concern, but it is not an issue.
Patches (either live, from flash memory or from sdcard) are (equally) loaded into RAM to run. Otherwise there would be a performance difference between patches loaded live, sdcard or flash.
SRAM and SDRAM do not have limited write/erase cycles.

Johannes
