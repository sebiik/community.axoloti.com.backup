Gavin:

And the rest of the bits 27 to 32 represents the values 1 to 64 giving the normal range number format hence the need to increase the calculating values by a magnitude of 5 over (1<<27) to get to 32. ?

1<<27 corresponds to 1
1<<28 corresponds to 2
1<<29 corresponds to 4
1<<30 corresponds to 8
1<<31 corresponds to 16 (unsigned) or -16 (signed)
