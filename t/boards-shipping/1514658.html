johannes 2016-02-24 16:49:30 UTC #33

Unfortunately the Belgian Post insists on 

Company Name
Surname Lastname
Streetname Housenumber Box
Postcode City
Country

while many countries put streetname before housenumber. To make things worse every field has a limited length.
For the USA I routinely move the housenumber into the streetname field, and put a dot in housenumber (it requires minimum one character...). Often I have to move parts of the streetname into the housenumber or box field because it goes over the length limit of the streetname. The structure does not seem to matter, as long as it prints right on the shipping label (which I check), except for the zipcode.
State is not needed, it's redundant with the zipcode. Parcels appear to be pre-sorted in Belgium and land in the right side of the USA for further processing.

I raised this issue with BPost a couple of times, but haven't seen any change so far.

But the good news: I can recall only a single parcel that bounced back shipping to the USA (and I don't remember why it bounced) - out of many many shipped to the USA.
