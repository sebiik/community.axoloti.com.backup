The Serge wave multiplier sections are based on heavy distortions (VCA / saturation, wavefolder, full wave rectifier) combined with filtering.
https://www.cgs.synth.net/modules/cgs113_vcm.html
If you do not plan to exactly model it, i think it is possible to do similar stuff with the axoloti.

It is a good challenge to implement these on the axoloti with limited aliasing.
