Hi,
Other users experienced the same in the past:

V1.2 board getting very hot when headphone jack is used

It's a known issue due to different voltage between the ground in headphone jack and other grounds in Axoloti.. you can cut the traces and divert the signal from the regular output or just use an adaptor in order to get the audio from the "proper" output
