If I were doing this, I would convert the float to a suitable range of ints (say 0-255 mapped for 8 bit) and then send it out on 8 pins to the axoloti, which would then read those 8 pin values to a 0-255 number. Seems a bit convoluted and not as elegant, but that's my two cents.
