This Patch is inspired by the Cranes patch of Norns by Monome

The heart of the patch is based on to independent Loopers wich can be triggered together or individually.
Playback can be reversed and forward with different speed settings (indiv. for each Looper)
Start- , Loop-, Stop- Points are implemented for each Looper
A global Reverb with Freeze + a LP-Filter is also on board
