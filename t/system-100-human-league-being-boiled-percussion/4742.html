Sputnki 2018-07-03 15:50:56 UTC #2

Are you using the counter object for the sequencer?
Consider that when triggered the counter object advances of 1 step, and the "default" state is 0.
The correct way to patch a sequencer takes this into account, and when you press play (and only in that occurrence) you should reset the counter (via the reset inlet) instead of triggering it.

Clear enough?
