Sputnki 2016-09-16 10:27:59 UTC #2

Granular synthesis. I made a couple objects about it, the problem is that for such a smooth playback a huge number of grains is required (and that requires more resources than axoloti actually has)
I also suspect that the window shape (the amplitude envelope for grains) is important to achieve a smoother sound. Again, the problem is that "curved" window shapes, like hanning, hamming, tapered cosine, welch, etc require more calculations than a simple triangular or rectangular shape.
