The Cyclone Analogic TT-606 has some cool additional features which require some button-combination presses to reach though. Fortunately they can also be controlled via MIDI, so I made this Axoloti controller, using my trusty Akai Midimix:

TTNM__TT-606_Control_v01_Description.jpeg1528x2124 1.71 MB

So we have mute buttons at our fingertips, control over tone and even instrument sound selection, shuffle, auto fill rate (I love this feature!) and individual "nuance" controls for almost all instruments. This lets us keep the TT-606 in sequencer mode, for pattern switching, chaining and editing, while the sound parameters are controlled with the Midimix.

I also included a crunchy distortion audio effect combo (just has a wet/dry know and a bitcrush amount knob) and two buttons for temporary low cut and/or hi cut.

You can download everything, including the Midimix mapping file, from my Axoloti GitHub:
