Gavin 2019-02-15 02:26:17 UTC #2

You do easily patch this. First connect your voltage to the Analog input (do not connect more then 3.3v or you will fry the analog input on the axo) then see what range comes through with the standard range of 64, so if say it is 12, then you can augment the values with maths etc to get the right mapping and triggers if you want to initiate midi events.
