mtyas 2017-05-16 12:34:14 UTC #3

The solenoids I use are 12v, and I don't use relay, but a home made simple circuit made with a transistor, a diode and a resistor.
You need to test your power supply by wiring up it up directly to a solenoid. If it moves, then the power supply and the solenoid isn't at fault.
I don't know what your relay module board is, and if it what kind of control voltage it requires, but if (as I read on the other thread) the leds light up, then I suppose it is working. Make sure all polarities are right, and that they work without using the relay (and that the power supply produces enough current).
