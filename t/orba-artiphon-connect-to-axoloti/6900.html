tele_player 2020-10-25 22:40:00 UTC #2

Axoloti recognizes the Orba, but Axoloti is looking for an interface with Class=1 (audio) and Subclass=3 (midistreaming).

monjet 2020-10-25 22:44:34 UTC #3
