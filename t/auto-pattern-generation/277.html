A pure Axoloti pattern generator with 8 bit pattern storage. Inspired by a discussion about the "Turing Machine Module" for Eurorack.

Per round a 8 bit pattern is generated and repeated 64 steps.

You can change the speed
You can reset the pattern (press a key on your midi keyboard)
You can press a button to decide "Create now the pattern"
You can change the intensity pauses are generated.
You can use sound presets 1,2,3....

A simple rhythem pattern is configured. Switch on off
And many more knobs to fiddle around and manipulate the pattern length and the sound and randomisation.

...and yes.. a little bit supersawing....

The tone generation was not the main intention (it's not so spectacular). The focus was more on "how to create a repeatable pattern".

Have fun... rungler_8.axp (27.5 KB)
