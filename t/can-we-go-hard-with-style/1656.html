i am currently working on a synth project, namely putting an axoloti synth in a nectar impact ix61 keyboard, the synth so far is mostly done, and here is a demo video of it in action:

anyone has a pinout for that specific keyboard? (nektar ix61)
i also contacted their support if they are willing to help me out. (they won't help me sadly)

anyhow,
current features of the patch:
each voice (5 voices):
- 1 supersquare (7 square osc) with detune amount and mix
- 2 supersaws (one of them is 1 octave higher)
- lowpass filter with adsr
- highpass filter with keytracking
- velocity sensitive input

1 global lfo on the voices' pitch input

4 band equalizer

mono delay with filter

simple reverb (thanks mtyas!) with a added low/highpass filter

compressor to prevent output overload

update on last edit (may 11 2016)

only applied a noise cancellation, see track details for the reason.

current DSP load: ~94%
current RAM usage: ~90%
