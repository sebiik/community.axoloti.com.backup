thetechnobear 2017-10-13 15:08:02 UTC #3

Saturate is only used on the top level patch, not subpatches. (we use the same dialog for main patch and subpatches)

generally within axoloti the audio processing the audio signal is done at 28 bits. this is then 'saturated' at the root level to 32 bits. normally this is fine, unless you are already saturating your signal due to your patching/processing.
