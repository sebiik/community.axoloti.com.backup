It's around 7 working days shipping on average to the USA, most significant uncertainty comes from customs processing. Orders are generally processed twice a week, your order would ship out on Monday. Please accept some margin on that, logistics becomes a mess if it arrives after you leave, I'd suggest to use an address of a friend who lives in the USA.
