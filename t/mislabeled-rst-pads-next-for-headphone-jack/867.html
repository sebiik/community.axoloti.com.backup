I can confirm they are mislabeled, however it is the T(ip) and R(ing) that are swapped, so still in a different order than the quarter inch jacks.
This affects both Axoloti Core revision v1.0 and v1.1.

In practice this is just the left and right channel swapped in only the silkscreen labels.
