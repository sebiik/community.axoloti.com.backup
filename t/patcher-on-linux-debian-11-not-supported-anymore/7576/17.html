I managed to "fix" this by modifying the file /opt/Axoloti/app/firmware/Makefile.patch by changing the line:
BDIR=$(subst $(SPACE),\ ,${axoloti_home}/build)

To instead simply say:
BDIR=${axoloti_home}/build

It's not elegant, and maybe there is a better way, but clearly this substitution is not correct with newer make version.
