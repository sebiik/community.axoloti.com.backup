I'm based in the US, but it's worth checking some of these large electronics suppliers EU shipping rates:

Mouser (most comprehensive, learning curve to navigate -- worth it, quality)
Tayda (cheap and easy to navigate for beginners -- not always the highest quality components, but fine most times)
Digikey
Allied Electronics
Jameco
