I've been experimenting with pushing the limits of traditional multiFX, this patch uses the midi note of the kick drum on MIDI channel 10 as a trigger for Sidechain compression. It also creates a pure sine sub-oscillator for some deep bass goodness. :3
I think the wonky nice sound of the SammichSID works out really well with the clean sound of the Digitakt and I'm really satisfied with how this track turned out. What do you think? =)
