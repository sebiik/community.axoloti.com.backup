johannes 2016-04-26 11:54:31 UTC #2

Yes they're not documented (except in the schematic).

They're provisions for Axoloti Control (X3), and audio clock synchronization (X4). Audio clock synchronization is required to create a digital audio link between multiple Axoloti Core boards. But so far this has not been implemented.
