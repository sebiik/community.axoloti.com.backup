johannes 2016-07-08 11:39:17 UTC #3

"Developer mode" detects the presence of a .git folder in the axoloti folder, if that's the case, it's supposedly a custom git clone and compile rather than an installed pre-compiled application. Developer mode unhides a few menu items that are useful for developers and potentially confusing for other users. That does not open access to your computer, no need to smile to your webcam
