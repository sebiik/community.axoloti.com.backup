Blindsmyth 2020-04-29 15:08:30 UTC #2

The pins labelled rts (input) rts (output) rst (headphones) are where you have to go.
r = ring = right
t = tip = left
s = sleeve = ground

Blindsmyth 2020-04-29 16:41:08 UTC #4

Yes that is correct! normally there the 2 pins are just doubled. You can however cut the connection between the two.

Then one row will connect to the jacks. The other row will connect to the ins and outs of the board. Like this you can repurpose the onboard jacks for other use.
