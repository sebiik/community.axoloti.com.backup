A little description of the realization of this machine.
The enclosure is a cardboard box. The d√©coration is mine ( done with ArtMatic Designer ).
In the box is the AXOLOTI CORE linked to the 2 pots that allow to regulate the gain of the left and right audio outs.
The micro SD card inclosed in the Axoloti contains the software I have created on my Mac.
It is quite simple. It is done of 2 equal parts that express themselves, one through the right channel and the other through the right channel.
Each of this parts is constituted of 6 oscillators modulated ( in frequency and gain ) by very slow LFO's.
The tone is changing all the time, very slowly.
You can balance the left and right channel with the 2 buttons.
