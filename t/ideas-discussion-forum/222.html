yeah, I was going to add a 'General Category' but feared it might mean posters just dumped everything in there... and then no one can remember where they saw that thread about X.

I thought that perhaps discussions specific to the hardware, software and patching might be better served being separated.
you can still post general topics in there, e.g. patching competitions in the patching category,
or an idea you've had for a hardware device etc... and of course ideas about the firmware/UI can be posted in the software category.

so post where you think is most appropriate, and if it becomes clearer we need a broader category I'll create one.
(its very tempting to create lots of categories as a moderator, but I think most users like to have as few as possible )

one thing to consider (and I don't have the numbers), a good percentage of the boards have now been shipped, but many will still be 'in shipping', even within Europe it will often be 5-7 days, and could be longer for other parts of the world.... and then as you know, Axoloti is pretty deep, so going to take a while for many to get their heads around it.
so perhaps wait a little, till people get their boards for competitions etc?

anyway just thoughts, feel free to throw ideas out there to the community and see what sticks
