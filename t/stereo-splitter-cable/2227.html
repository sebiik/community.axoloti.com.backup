johannes 2016-09-10 09:18:16 UTC #6
Chambers79:

I have plenty of mono cables. And haven't tried those as I believe I'd get distortion.

That was an issue with board revision 1.0 and is fixed on later revisions. You can find the board revision near the edge of the board close to the audio input jack.
Stereo to dual mono 1/4" cables are often called "insert cables".
