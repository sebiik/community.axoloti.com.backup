While I wouldn't call this Hacking I would call this a clever use of built-in features of the MIDI protocol

You'll find that although MIDI CC's are conveniently there for you that their 7-bit resolution (only 127 discrete values are available!) may be a limiting factor. Some synths will be made nicely to prevent users from hearing stepping when the CC value changes while lots won't, too. Not sure about the Minilogue.
