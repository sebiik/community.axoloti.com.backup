Well in short, I made a patch that lets you use two notes on your Midi device to cycle through a selection of effects in a patchbank. What I did not think of was that you need to include this patch as a subpatch in every patch in the patchbank otherwise you are only able to choose the first effect. I can elaborate when I am at my studio again, now that I figured it out it seems so obvious I can't see how I overlooked it in the first place
