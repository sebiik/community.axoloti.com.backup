Here's a noodle I spent some time crafting. It's on it's way to becoming a full-out ambient patch.

For now it does some arpeggiating of the brds plucked osc fed into a chorus, there's a pad configured in a chord by three brds wavetable oscs. Both are wired to an fx send passed to the rngs reverb.

Nothing is wired to knobs or inputs.

All that's required is to load the patch - it will start on it's own. The LFO in the top left controls the main tempo. Change the DIV params on the clocked LFOs for some changes to the "song structure"

Lots of other things to tell you about in the patch but I must sleep.
