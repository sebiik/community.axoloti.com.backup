Gavin 2017-09-06 01:14:33 UTC #4

Have found that the following connections are required to connect the Arduino Due serial uart to Axoloti midi input din.

Arduino Due TX pin ------------------- Axoloti Midi in din pin 5
Arduino Due 3.3v ----220ohm-R---- Axoloti Midi in din pin 4
Arduino Due GND --------------------- Axoloti Midi in din pin 2
