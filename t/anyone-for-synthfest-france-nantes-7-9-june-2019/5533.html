SmashedTransistors 2019-05-12 12:57:20 UTC #7

The patch is quite specific to the Akai APC25key and is still under development. Yep, it is a 2"42 OLED, i will use it as a parameter "reminder" the 1"3 Oled is mainly for scope.

The patch is essentially based on the sync/formant phase modulation oscillators tiar/osc/SyncPM2 and the tiar/dist/SyncAlias object to add some old digital flavor.
