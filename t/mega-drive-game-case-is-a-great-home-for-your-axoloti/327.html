Hi everyone,
Just wanted to share that the Axoloti fits perfectly in an old Mega Drive game case (at least an Australian one). It also lines up really well with the plastic clamps that hold the game cartridge, and would be usefull with a bit of stanley knife cutting to provide good anchors to hold the Axoloti in place. Additionally, the case is easy to cut to expose all the external connections. Finally, it adds Retro charm. All the best = )
