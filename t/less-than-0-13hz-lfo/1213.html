anon5189335 2016-03-04 16:05:38 UTC #3

Have you tried connecting a dial/b to the pitch inlet of the lfo? If you do that, setting the dial/b to MINUS 12 will make the lfo speed half of what is set on the lfo itself. So if the lfo speed is 0.13hz it will be 0,065 hz. Setting it to -24 = 0,0325 hz... and so on.... I think that could work too.

Or else I'd suggest the lin lfo too.
