No way for me to test right now, but there's a thread Matt started with the aim of generating a complete GM soundset for Axoloti, created using Axoloti. There's a way to go yet until it's complete, but even at this early stage I notice there's synth drums uploaded:

General Midi collaborative library Challenges
I’ve decided to start a topic / challenge for the creation of a collaborative “General Midi” library. The aim is to have a bunch of patches, easy to use, and useful to learn understand basic axoloti patching. It would be great to cover all the main sounds included in the GM 1 sound set, but it’s only names, so we’re very free to do whatever it inspires us. It’s not very important to be realistic, everybody has probably very different visions of what a synthesized acoustic patch may sound like,…
