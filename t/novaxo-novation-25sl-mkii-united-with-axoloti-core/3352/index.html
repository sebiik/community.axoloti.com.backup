I would like to introduce my first project NovAxo. It was a great joy to unite Axoloti with the midi controller.
I have also installed 6 potentiometers, 8 Leds, 4 pushbuttons and a Sharp distance sensor in the Novation 25SL MkII.
The X-Y-Pad is hard to use and works only with a lot of pressure.

I connect Axoloti and Novation Controller over the Midi connection, since I both operate over 9 V at the same time.
On the patch I am constantly working on. It is a 3-voice poly synth with the following functions:

  • Multi OSC with Shape and FM from Sine Sub OSC. Noise OSC and Super Saw
  • 3 LFO with Multishape, BPM Sync and DAW/Midi Sync
  • 2 Env
  • Step Sequencer, 16 steps with load/save
  • FX Chorus and Reverb
  • Modulation of Cutoff, Shape, FM, Pitch, LFO1 Rate

My problem is the restriction of SRAM. Because I am constantly at the limit. CPU is not a problem. My Patch is a mess.
