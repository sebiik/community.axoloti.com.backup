Hey Dave, what you are looking for is known as a hook, the one I use is TSG/ctrl/MultiPot 3, this means you can use one pot to control 3 separate controls or layers, then use a counter & toggle switch to select between the layers in hardware or a radio object in the patcher

http://www.privatepublic.de/public/community-objectlist.html#TSGctrl
