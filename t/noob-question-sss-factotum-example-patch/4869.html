the factotum doesn't really need a help-patch.
simply record (toggle to record) audio & play with the parameters.
the problems (no sound e.g.) arises out of a combination of parameters,
it can do sooo much, that sometimes problems seem to appear, like
no pitch-shifting if window = 0
or rate = 1 = normal speed, rate = 0 would be no speed/sound...
one has to learn the factotum like an instrument.

let me know what your problems are if you not solved them yet.
