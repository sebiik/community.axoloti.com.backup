Moutcast 2018-05-17 08:51:23 UTC #3

several people have ask me for the patch. here it comes:
table seqeuncing slow.axp (29.6 KB)

first of all sorry for the chaos. but i was still changing things on it around...
it has 4 voices playing (4 oscillators). for each of the 4 voices a random number sequence is recorded into a table (the range and spread can be changed). the recording is triggered by lfo. after recording the sequence is repeated untill the next trigger. a new random sequence is played into the table. sometimes only part of the old sequence is overwritten. the numbers coming out of the tables are then quantized to the scale i want.
