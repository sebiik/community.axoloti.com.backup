UHF 2019-04-30 01:38:06 UTC #2

According to page 3 of the schematic, it's a side-view green LED with a 2.2V forward voltage (Vf) and it's very, very small. Anything similar with same Vf will do, but it's designed to be seen from the side. A not-so-small smd LED could be soldered on it's side (rather than flat as normal) to achieve the same thing probably, the pads look wide enough to accommodate something bigger. I don't know where you live and might order from, but cheap smd LEDs are available from eBay (example). You could order from a proper distributor like Digikey, Mouser, RS... but there are minimum order quantities and shipping costs.

The orange thing that also got bashed is a capacitor (looking at my board I think it's C65 and the working of the Axoloti should be ok.) Otherwise, don't worry if everything else is working!
