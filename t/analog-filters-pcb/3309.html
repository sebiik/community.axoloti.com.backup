geecen 2017-05-10 09:04:56 UTC #2

That is not a generic analogue filter board, it is made to work with the Shruthi. It's also monophonic, so you would just be able to have one filter over the whole sound, not one per note/voice as in a poly synth. With those caveats, you can still do it. I put my Axo through my shruthi filter often. You can then control the cutoff an resonance from the Axo midi out.

See this quick and bad video I did showing me doing it: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yemkXAwvel4
