SmashedTransistors 2017-03-02 17:55:19 UTC #2

It's simply a board.

The strict minimum is such a USB / micro USB cord and a headphone.

You can connect it to a MIDI controller or keyboard though a MIDI cord or USB.

You can add a microSD card.

The output and inputs are stereo 1/4" stereo cords. You may need some "insert cables" to convert these to pairs of mono jacks.
