Bastl's 60 Knobs controller is out as a DIY kit. Looks like a good candidate for an Axo controller/enclosure with a bit of modding. The device has a microcontroller with software editor and holds 5 presets. Make a patch in Axoloti, assign CC's, Get the blank case files and label them along with your patch, mod the enclosure to hold your Axo and wire it up internally over Midi and Wallah!

http://www.bastl-instruments.com/instruments/sixtyknobs/
