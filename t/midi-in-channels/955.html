janvantomme 2016-01-27 13:20:39 UTC #4

It's indeed possible if you use multiple subpatches in your patch.
For each subpatch, you can to check the **Has MIDI channel attribute" checkbox.
An extra dropdown for the MIDI channel will be displayed when you add the subpatch to your main patch.
