johannes 2016-10-18 22:46:15 UTC #4

MusicHackDay Berlin was great!
Meeting @Blindsmyth, @rbrt, Sylwester, and many others
Cooperating with @Hugo, Philippe and Anton on the development of a Axoloti object physical model of a clamped bar.
Then taping headphones (as a contact microphone, nothing else available) to the bottom of a food plate to pick up the contact sounds, to inject to that into the clamped bar resonator.
Submitting this project to the jury just-in-time, named "food|bar"
Presenting this to the jury as a game changer in new instruments for musical expression.
Winning the MusicHackDay "WTF Award"
We haven't received our prices yet, so can't review those yet:
