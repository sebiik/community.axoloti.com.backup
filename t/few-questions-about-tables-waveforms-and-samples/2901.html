Do you have a picture of your patch?
You have to name the table according to your memory allocation!!
I also work a lot with samples.
You should know that Axoloti searches the samples on the SD card in a specified folder.
If you are working with your Axo connected to your computer and you go live with a patch named 'untitled' it searches for the sample on the location SDCARD/untitled/ If you upload the patch on the SDcard as starup, it will search the samples on the ROOT of the SDcard, and NOT in /untitled/
I have no issues with not finding samples...
