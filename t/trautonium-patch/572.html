Made a little Trautonium-like patch.

It's kind-a-like the Doepfer Trautonium project. It has four subharmonic oscillators with divisor values from 1 to 8 and a resonant filter bank with four parallel filters, switchable between bandpass or lowpass. And for some more spice you can add a portamento glide and a mod-wheel vibrato.

Use lots of reverb on your mix (preferably plate or spring-like)! Axoloti rocks!
