Been working on this for a few months now and finally reached a jammable stage!
It's a 9-track sequenced sampler split across 2 axolotis, 6 drum parts on axo1 and 3 parts for stabs/bass/misc samples on axo2.
Patterns are preprogrammed across 3 sequencers and selectable for each part via pots - in the future I'd like to work on implementing exchangeable banks of patterns for each sequencer, but I struggle with tables.
Each axo has a one-knob LP+HP filter, a reverb, plus 2 distortion effects on axo1.
