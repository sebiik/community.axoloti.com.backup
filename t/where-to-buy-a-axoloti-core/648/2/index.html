AdrianWillaert 2015-11-30 16:05:39 UTC #17

I remember that the axoloti sold during kickstarter was
16bit / 48kHz but now on the product specs I read 24 bit / 96kHz

is this an upgrade or am I wrong?

Thanks!

johannes 2015-11-30 16:32:50 UTC #18

Axoloti Core is using a 24/96 capable audio converter at 24/48, it is not an upgrade. The only reason 24/96 is not exposed as an option is that it takes quite some work on the editor, firmware and objects to allow multiple sample rates, and patches may not sound the same at different sample rates.
So it says "24bit/96kHz capable stereo audio ADC/DAC", technically correct for the hardware spec, but maybe misleading. I added "(editor and firmware currently only supports 24bit/48kHz)" to it.

In my experience most 24/96kHz capable ADC/DAC's sound good at lower sample rates too.
