Hey!

Have been preparing S*** loads of loops for Axoloti over the weekend and noticed something nice I thought I'd share:

If you set the tempo of you DAW to 175,7813 bpm and render 4 bars you get an audiofile that id 262144 samples long. Which fits PERFECTLY with Axolotis tablesizes:

At 175,7813 BPM, X bars are equivalent to:

1 bar = 65536 samples
2 bar = 131072 samples
4 bar = 262144 samples
8 bar = 524288 samples
16 bar = 1048576 samples
32 bar = 2097152 samples
64 bar = 4194304 samples

This can shave of a few steps of preparing audioloops for Axoloti.

Hope you find it useful
