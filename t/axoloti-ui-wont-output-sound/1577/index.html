johannes 2016-04-26 21:32:03 UTC #2

All sound will come from the Axoloti Core board, not from your computer's (or soundcard) audio plugs.
Axoloti Core's audio performance is also independent of your computer's performance.
The Axoloti UI itself is not capable of generating any sound.
