Since the community is very new, and there aren't a lot of resources available to get started with patching, I've decided to write a book about Axoloti.

The book will teach you how to create basic synthesizers, drum machines and audio effects. These are the chapters I've planned. The first chapter is available as a free sample.

  • Chapter 1: Creating Your First Synthesizer
  • Chapter 2: Creating a Polyphonic Synthesizer
  • Chapter 3: Diving into Sound Design
  • Chapter 4: Creating Drum Machines
  • Chapter 5: Creating Audio Effects
  • Chapter 6: Adding Physical Controls

The book will be self-published via Leanpub. Working without a publisher will allow me to keep the book more affordable.

Enough talking for now. Get the book over here: https://leanpub.com/getting-started-with-axoloti/
