Hello, I have made a sine LFO which outputs four arbitrarily adjustable phase sine waves for purposes of modulation, and various experimentations. Each of its individual outputs can have an arbitrary phase angle(however they will all have the same frequency) It can be used as a quadrature low frequency oscillator, or you can use multipliers to mix the signals into complex waveforms.
If anyone could give me advice on how to make it into an audio-rate device i would be very pleased also.PhaseGeneratorLFO.axs (6.7 KB)

If you find any bugs please let me know.
There was a bug but the recent update solved it, it now works fine in polyphonic patches
