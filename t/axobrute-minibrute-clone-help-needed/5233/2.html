Hello everyone! I´m a new user of Axoloti. I´m tryng to make an emulation of Arturia Minibrute monophonic analog synth. It´s a really enjoyable instrument and I think it´s a good way of learning basics of synth design.
I´m playing it with a midi keyboard and korg nanokontrol. The parameters are mapped via midi mapping.
At the moment I have reached:

  • 4 Osc
    -Sine (subosc with Oct + - control)
    -Supersaw
    -Square
    -Noise
  • OSC Mixer
  • Filter 36db (SSS LPF) thanks @SirSickSik for this great module!
  • Envelope ADSR + LFO (interesting Env + LFO module from @SirSickSik )
  • Glide
  • Modulation Matrix : LFO to Amp, Cutoff, Pitch

Looks like you're off to a great start. I've never tried a tap tempo so someone else will have to help you there, but connecting an envelope generator to your filter is easy enough. Have a look at my Minimoog patch, there's an example of what I did there.

Moog Minimoog patch! Patching
Sharing for your enjoyment my latest patch... Minimoog. (Actually, more like the Minimoog Re-issue or Boog, because I included the LFO.) Controls are set to emulate Rick Wakeman's 'Catherine' patch (as per The Lengend's version). Controls mostly match those of the Minimoog, but in if you try to copy a MM patch, you'll find the knob positions don't necessarily correspond to the real thing. Cheers!Minimoog.axp (58.1 KB)

A modmatrix can get out of control really fast in Axoloti (at least I'm not sure of a hood way to do one), but have a look at a 'modsource'. They can come in handy.
