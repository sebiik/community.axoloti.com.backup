You mean, routing MIDI through Axoloti for the Nord Modular "PC" editor midi connection? I believe that requires SysEx support, while Axoloti does not support SysEx. Better use a regular USB-Midi interface connected to your PC/Mac for that.

Sending MIDI notes/controllers/clock from Axoloti to the Nord Modular over a DIN Midi cable should work fine.

johannes 2016-03-13 15:35:03 UTC #9

Ah, you need to have a positive value on the velo inlet of midi/note/out, without connection it has a zero value. A midi note-on with velocity zero is really a note-off by the midi standard.

johannes 2016-03-13 16:47:04 UTC #14

MIDI continuous controllers do not have a velocity. Value zero for a CC does not change the meaning.

It's only "note on" with velocity zero that is specified to mean "note off".
