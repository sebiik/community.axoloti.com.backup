I tried very quickly with an app called Midi Keyboard (here: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mobileer.midikeyboard&hl=en). Plug your phone to the Axoloti (big USB port). Then you have to choose "Use device as MIDI" in the Android System USB notification of your phone. Then, in the app, in the "Receiver for keys" menu, choose "Android USB Peripheral" and it should work.
