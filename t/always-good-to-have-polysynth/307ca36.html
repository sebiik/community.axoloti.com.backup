Hi folks.

Wanted to share my simple virtual analogue polysynth patch which - as I think - comes always handy. This patch offers three mixable waveforms (tri, saw, square with pwm), a square wave sub-oscillator, blendable low-pass/high-pass filters with filter envelope modulation (positive and negative) and - for fatness - a chorus at the end.

I tried to design the patch as tweakable as possible. The MIDI CC mappings are for my Edirol controller keyboard, but you can change all the CC numbers to match your setup.

I really like the fat sound little Axoloti can accomplish with such a simple patch! Spent several Vangeliesque moments with that... hope you like it!

Peter
