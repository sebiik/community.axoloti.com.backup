It's been three months of intense patching with Axoloti and finally I've got my sequenced sampler patch ready to go on the road and hopefully on stage this summer! Thanks to all of you that helped me, the input of this community is really great!

It is kind of a sampler that records little samples of my playing, triggers them from a step sequencer that I patched, plays the samples back in different octaves and/or reverse and puts it through filter and amp envelopes. In the end everything goes through a delay.

I control it using a nanokontrol 2 and I added jacks for Guitar Footswitches and Expression Pedals. The sound you here is purely axoloti, no external fx added. (really shitty recording, I want to change that in the future!) For guitar I want to add a preamp to make it sound more like an electric guitar, but this is already quite expressive.

Since it's nice weather here I want to play with it on the streets. I also plan to integrate this into a bigger more professional live setup, so If you're in Germany maybe you can catch me playing on a festival, the one that starts with and f and ends with usion for example
