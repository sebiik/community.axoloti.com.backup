DazzlingDuke 2016-08-03 16:47:14 UTC #1

Hi everyone! I wanted to let you know that I'll be having a booth at http://knobcon.com/ this year. Chicago is my home turf, so I should be bringing at least 6 prototypes and some surprises :).

The event is bonkers cool. I guarantee you will see something that will surprise or inspire you at the event. My favorite from last year was a eurorack controller for a servo, using CV controls to create physical rhythm instruments.

If anyone wants to have their Axoloti projects featured, I'm making a poster highlighting how awesome this community is. If you want to have your gear physically present, I'm happy to receive it, take extremely good care of it, demonstrate it, plug any info you want, and then ship it back. If you're fortunate enough to be in the area, I have a 10x10' booth and still have room, gratis. If you're just there to spectate, please stop by. I really enjoyed meeting people at the SF Maker Faire.

I'll be showing off the Believotron Wanderlust controller and will be launching a public beta at the event.
