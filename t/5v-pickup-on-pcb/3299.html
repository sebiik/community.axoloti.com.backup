Actually there is, you know the spot where you can cut away the midi section from the rest of the axo, then there are the points you can join once separated, two of these points are 5V and GND, I have used them before to power a device and it worked perfectly. I don't have my details with me, but there is a post somewhere that confirms which point is which.

Gavin 2017-05-09 02:48:21 UTC #3

Forgot to add, yes the 5v works when supplied via the USB micro input.
In fact I had once the Axo powered by the usb micro, tapped into the 5v for powering a midi controller, then used the usb hub to power an external effects unit that could be powered by USB.
It all worked perfectly.
