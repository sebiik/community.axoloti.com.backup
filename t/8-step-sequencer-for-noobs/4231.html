It maybe doesnt fall under the 8 steps sequencer category, but some of the best sequencers around that comes in a finished project, in my opinion at least, are Elektron sequencers. Like Digitakt or Octatrack for example.

So if you want some inspiration for sequencer use, check out their tutorials for their products. Read the manuals. They got their famous "P-lock" for example. Which means parameter lock and it means you can assign different parameter values to each step of the sequencer. Which opens up a whole new ball game

Anyway, check them out, they are really inspiring products when it comes to sequencing
