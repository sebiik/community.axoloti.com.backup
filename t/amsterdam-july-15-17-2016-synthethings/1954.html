Amsterdam, July 15-17 2016, Synthethings

News/Events
dreamer 2016-07-05 09:23:29 UTC #1

Meetup for those interested in diy synthesizers, noise machines and audio gear.
There is room for discussion, presentations and hands-on tinkering.
Expect some soldering, patching, tweaking, and noodling of audio waves that tickle your ear.

This month we present 3 days of playing with audio stuff:
Friday 15th: a meetup about the old Formant renovation project and diy synths
Saturday 16th: patching circle -> Puredata, MAX/MSP, Axoloti: patch it all up!
Sunday 17th: making/breaking/playing with all kinds of audio projects -> bring your own!
