janvantomme 2017-06-22 15:09:43 UTC #2

You'll need a keyboard and a pitch bend object. Best is to feed the pitch bend through a math/*c object before adding it to the note signal of the keyboard. Setting the math/*c object to 12.00 will give you a full octave bend below and above the played note. This will work with a regular keyboard. Can't give you advice on MPE since I don't own a device.
