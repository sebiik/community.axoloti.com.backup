My first patch and although it's a basic bread & butter polyphonic OB-X inspired brass patch, I am pleasantly surprised with how it's turned out so far and I thought I'd share it with the community.

A couple of features worth mentioning:
8 voice polyphony
Stereo out with auto voice panning
Pitch and mod wheel input
uses TSG's fine reverb (so, if you prefer an external reverb, you can get more voices).

As I'm a NOOB and have a lot to learn here, please feel free to suggest and share improvements and suggestions...

and Enjoy!
