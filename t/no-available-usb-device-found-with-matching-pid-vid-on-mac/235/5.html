johannes 2016-06-18 21:43:17 UTC #14
wishniak:

the lights flash while im restarting + holding S1

The LEDs do not blink at all when entering rescue ("dfu") mode, follow exactly these steps to enter rescue mode:

  1. Remove power from Axoloti Core
  2. Push&hold button S1
  3. Plug in USB (to your computer)
  4. Only now you can release button S1
  5. Start a firmware flash (rescue) in the board menu.
