So I've embedded an Axoloti into my KeyStep. The job's pretty rough as I didn't have access to power tools at the time. It's great to have something I can carry around wherever I go. The tube goes to a breath sensor that I soldered up so I can use my breath instead of an envelope. You can even hum into it for amplitude modulation! It's loosely based off of mitxela's project here: https://mitxela.com/projects/breath_controller_mk_ii
