My patch collection will be shared in this post.

LEAD - rebourne:

Description: the aim was to let it sound like the hardstyle producer: "rebourne" does his synths, and to be honest, i'm quite satisfied with just one axoloti in making the sound.

Features: it has multiple oscillators, 3 polyvoice, uses ~90% DSP, nearly all the ram, and has a massive sound
ld_rebourne.axp (111.0 KB), made in Axoloti version: 1.0.10

LEAD - Hardstyler:

Description: the aim was to let it sound like a nice stereo hardstyle lead. play with the filter sustain to fade between a pluck and a lead

Features: it has multiple saw, supersaw and square oscillators, 4 polyvoice, uses ~78% DSP, ~30k ram, and has a massive stereo sound. also included is my 'soundgood' module to fatten it up.
hardstyler.axp (46.9 KB), made in Axoloti version: 1.0.10

LEAD - PHD:

Description: the aim was to let it sound like the song "I won't let you down" by PH.D.

Features: it has 2 supersaws and one regular saw oscillators per voice, 6 polyvoice, uses ~94% DSP, nearly all the ram, and has a close to original sound.
PHD.axp (101.3 KB), made in Axoloti version: 1.0.10

INSTRUMENT - Metal Guitar:

Description: the aim was to create a nice sounding metal-like guitar with FM and distortions.

Features: it has quite some FM going on and alot of distortion, thus allowing only 2 voices, but sounds rather nice for 'just' 168MHZ of DSP power. uses ~95% DSP and nearly all the ram.
guitar.axp (33.3 KB), made in Axoloti version: 1.0.10

INSTRUMENT - Dark panflute:

Description: a simple, nice sounding panflute, soundas a little on the dark side.

Features: it has quite some square osc's going on, eq and some reverb, thus allowing 5 voices, but sounds rather nice. uses ~88% DSP and nearly all the ram.

panflute_dark.axp (99.2 KB), made in Axoloti version: 1.0.10
