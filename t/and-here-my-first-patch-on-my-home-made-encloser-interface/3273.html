So here is my first patch played entirely on the Axolti Core and its home made enclosure/interface. Using in this case Potentiometers on 11 of the Analog GPIO input pins of the Axoloti.
One of he many things that makes me so excited about the Axoloti board is the possibilty to tune it to whatever. I have come to find the usual equal temperament of most of the synth's a bit exausted. In this patch i have tuned all sounds exactly to the tuning of the populaer Koshi Chimes (Aqua Tuning Flavour):-). Please let me know what you think...

6 knobs are controlling the decay of the sounds, 1 knob is adding fm/sine wave to the sounds, another knob is a low pass filter. with two knobs i am changing the rythm patterns of the sounds and the percussion. another knob controlls a low pass filter on the percs.
Recorded into Ableton
