janvantomme 2021-05-13 09:22:42 UTC #2

Some quick ideas, haven't tested them since I don't have an axoloti with extra hardware on my desk now.

  1. Use the button with a counter to toggle a mux or demux object on/off. Can also be used with a mux 8 for instance to select between 8 signals.

  2. Use a pot to toggle a mux object.

  3. Use a pot to control a mix/xfade object to mix between dry wet, or between different effects.
