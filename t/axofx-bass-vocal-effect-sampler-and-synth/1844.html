I'd like to share progress of my project with Axoloti. I've started it at the end of March and I'm extremely slow at it .

So, this was how it started: http://community.axoloti.com/t/anyone-planning-to-make-and-sell-an-enclosure/140/194?u=igor

What I'm trying to build is one device that will replace three devices I'm currently using with my band: Korg Kaossilator, Roland Voice Transformer and sample player I've built (http://hyperglitch.com/articles/stmpler).

It should process my microphone (two delays, lo-fi effect), my bass guitar (distortion, filter with LFO), play samples triggered by MIDI and be some sort of synth. Currently I have vocal effects partly done and sample player (this: http://community.axoloti.com/t/configurable-sd-card-sample-player-object/1843?u=igor).

UI consists of five pots, six buttons and eight LEDs connected directly to Axoloti. Small keyboard, rotary encoder, touchpad (not yet connected) and port for external sensors are connected to Arduino which also handles OLED display and communicates with Axoloti via UART.
