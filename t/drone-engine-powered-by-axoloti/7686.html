I had this Axoloti core for a couple of years and never gave good use to it, until now
I've assembled a patch with two Mutable Instruments Braids oscilators and a third generic oscillator, going into a mixer, a delay and a reverb.
I also have some potentiometers modules and multiplexers from past projects, connected to a Teensy LC sending MIDI to the Axo.
The pitch potentiometers should be connected to a analog inputs, instead of midi, to have full resolution

I would like to thank everyone who contributes to this community, because without this forum I wouldn't be able to make this.
I will leave the patch here if anyone wants to play with it.
